HanapLink is a community-driven digital platform dedicated to improving the reporting and recovery process for missing persons through technology and collaboration. The platform serves as a centralized and secure system where families, community members, and authorized agencies can report cases, share verified information, and receive real-time updates.

Committed to responsible and ethical data handling, HanapLink incorporates privacy and security measures, including role-based access and information verification mechanisms, to ensure that sensitive data is protected while enabling effective community participation.

💡

Vision

To become the leading community-driven platform that reunites missing individuals with their loved ones through collaboration, trust, and innovation.

🎯

Mission

To use technology to unite communities and authorities in finding missing persons through secure, real-time, and verified collaboration.
